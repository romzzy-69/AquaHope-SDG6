Our Mission

Water is life. Access to clean water and sanitation is a basic human right, yet billions of people worldwide lack these essential services. Through AquaHope, we raise awareness about UN Sustainable Development Goal 6 and work toward ensuring everyone has access to safely managed water and sanitation services.

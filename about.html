Our Story

AquaHope was born from a shared vision among Sri Lankan students who recognized the critical importance of water and sanitation access for sustainable development. Inspired by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 6, we set out to create a platform that would educate, inform, and inspire action toward achieving clean water and sanitation for all by 2030.

Our name "AquaHope" represents the hope for universal water security ‚Äì each step being a movement forward in awareness, education, and action. Just as water flows and connects communities, we aim to connect people with the knowledge and tools they need to contribute to this global goal.

Through research, data visualization, and accessible content, we're working to make the complex challenges of water and sanitation understandable and actionable for students, communities, and decision-makers alike.

What is SDG 6?

Sustainable Development Goal 6 is one of the 17 Global Goals that make up the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. It calls for clean water and sanitation for all people, no matter where they live or what their circumstances.

Access to water, sanitation and hygiene is a human right, and yet billions are still faced with daily challenges accessing even the most basic of services. Around 2 billion people still do not have safely managed drinking water at home and 3.6 billion people ‚Äì nearly half the global population ‚Äì lack safely managed sanitation.

The COVID-19 pandemic demonstrated the critical importance of sanitation, hygiene and adequate access to clean water for preventing and containing diseases. Handwashing is one of the most effective actions you can take to reduce the risk of disease.

SDG 6 Targets

Eight specific targets to achieve by 2030

6.1 Universal Access to Safe Drinking Water

By 2030, achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all.

6.2 Universal Access to Sanitation

By 2030, achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all and end open defecation, paying special attention to the needs of women and girls and those in vulnerable situations.

6.3 Improve Water Quality

By 2030, improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals and materials.

6.4 Water-Use Efficiency

By 2030, substantially increase water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensure sustainable withdrawals and supply of freshwater.

6.5 Water Resource Management

By 2030, implement integrated water resources management at all levels, including through transboundary cooperation as appropriate.

6.6 Protect Water Ecosystems

By 2020, protect and restore water-related ecosystems, including mountains, forests, wetlands, rivers, aquifers and lakes.

6.A International Cooperation

By 2030, expand international cooperation and capacity-building support to developing countries in water- and sanitation-related activities and programmes.

6.B Community Participation

Support and strengthen the participation of local communities in improving water and sanitation management.
